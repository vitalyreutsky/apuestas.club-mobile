Betway security and licenses

There are multiple security measures in place to protect all customers on the site. First of all, the website uses 256-bit SSL encryption provided by Thawte to make sure that all data is kept safe. This applies to personal details when registering and banking details when depositing. Also, Betway uses several firewalls for even better protection against unauthorised access. The operator is licensesd and regulated by the Malta Gaming Authority and by the Gibraltar Gambling Commissioner.
